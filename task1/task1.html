Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Definition: Artificial Intelligence refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think and learn like humans.

Key Areas of AI

  • Machine Learning
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Computer Vision
  • Robotics

Real-World Applications

AI is used in voice assistants, self-driving cars, recommendation systems, fraud detection, and more.
